What is Coronavirus?

  • Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.
  • Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment.
  • Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes and cancer are likely to develop illness.

Symptoms of Coronavirus

High Fever

A high fever is when the body temperature rises above 103 degrees Fahrenheit in an adult (or above 101 degrees Fahrenheit in a child).

Cough

A cough is your body's way of responding when something irritates your throat or airways. An irritant stimulates nerves that send a message to your brain.

Sore Troath

A sore throat is pain, scratchiness or irritation of the throat that often worsens when you swallow.

Headache

Headaches are a common health problem — most people experience them at some time.
